Connect your ESPN league

Three steps, about two minutes. Nothing here reads or sends anything on its own — you stay in control of what gets shared and when.

01

Download the tool

A small Windows program that checks your browser for your existing ESPN login — it doesn't ask for your password.

Download get_espn_credentials.exe
02

Run it

Double-click the downloaded file. If Windows shows an "unrecognized publisher" warning, click More info → Run anyway — that's expected for a small unsigned tool like this.

You'll see something like this:

Looking for ESPN Fantasy login cookies in your browsers...

Found credentials in Chrome!

SWID: {1A2B3C4D-...}
espn_s2: AEB3x9f...

Copy both values above and send them back as instructed.
03

Send the two values back

Paste them in below, then hit send — it opens your email client with everything filled in already.
